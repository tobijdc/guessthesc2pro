    How to play:

    Guess the SC2 Pro is all about guessing a SC2 player. You will get hints initially from which you need to determine the player.

    Once you make a guess, if it is wrong, you will see what mismatched compared to the mystery player. Fo reference you can always expand the "Player List" in the bottom, it is sorted by tournament winnings.

    Guess correctly within the allowed number of guesses, you win. Play again by pressing "Reset Game".
